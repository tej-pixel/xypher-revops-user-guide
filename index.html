1
Acquisition

Awareness

A new lead enters your world. Your only job is to capture who they are — the system immediately starts a tracked lifecycle and attributes the source.

👤What you do
  • Create or own a new lead — web form, event list, or manual entry.
  • Fill the essentials: name, company, title, lead source.
  • Nothing else — no manual staging, no scoring by hand.
⚙️What the system does for you
Lead — Set Default Lifecycle Stage
Stamps the lead at “1 – Inquiry” automatically so it enters the funnel cleanly.
Lifecycle — Create & Link (Lead)
Spins up a dedicated Lifecycle record and links it — this is the spine that tracks every stage date from here on.
Lead — Firmographic Banding
Converts raw employee count into a clean Employee Band (e.g. 1000+).
Lead — Scorecard Auto-Assignment
Attaches the active ICP Scorecard so grading can run.
Lead Attribution Engine
Creates a Target Attribution record crediting the campaign / source that generated the lead.
Lead — Foundation Governance
Enforces clean status values and initialises foundational fields on create.
🔎 Look for: the Lifecycle lookup populated on the lead, the Lead Source = Webinar, and an Employee Band already filled — all three appeared without you touching them.
2
Acquisition

Education

The lead is nurtured and continuously scored. The system tells you how good a fit this is and how engaged they are — so you spend time on the right people.

👤What you do
  • Log activities, calls and emails as you nurture.
  • Read the ICP Grade and BANT Score to prioritise.
  • Advance the lead to MQL when marketing-qualified.
⚙️What the system does for you
ICP Firmographic & Technographic Grade
Grades industry, size & tech-stack fit A–D from the lead’s firmographics.
ICP Persona Grade
Matches the job title against your ICP persona map.
ICP Composite & Scorecard Grade
Blends all sub-grades into a single ICP Grade + label (e.g. “Hot ICP”).
Lead — BANT Score Calculation
Weighs Budget · Authority · Need · Timeline into a 0–100 BANT Score.
Lifecycle Stage Engine + SLA Start scheduled support
Stamps milestone dates as the lead moves Inquiry → Engaged → MQL, and starts a 2-day SLA clock at MQL.
Behavioural & Grade Recalculation nightly
Re-scores engagement (activities, campaigns, intent, web visits) every night so grades never go stale.

3
Acquisition

Selection

Sales accepts the hand-off and qualifies the deal. The lead converts into an Opportunity, and the system carries every scrap of context across so nothing is re-typed.

👤What you do
  • Accept the SQL hand-off and convert the lead.
  • Use the “New Opportunity” guided button for fast, clean creation.
  • Run discovery; fill MEDDPICC and set the Next Step.
⚙️What the system does for you
Lead BDR → AE Hand-off Notification async
Emails the AE the moment the lead hits SQL and sets an acceptance-due date.
New Opportunity Intake guided flow
The “New Opportunity” button resolves the account, creates contacts and links contact roles in one guided screen.
Lead Conversion Data Stamping + Lifecycle Sync
Copies source, BANT and lineage from the lead onto the new Contact & Opportunity.
Opportunity — Name Stamp
Auto-builds a structured name: Account – Deal Type – Product – Region – Quarter.
MEDDPICC + BANT Score & Carry-Over
Scores qualification 0–100 and carries the lead’s BANT answers into the deal.
Pipeline Entry & Stage Velocity Stamp
Marks when the deal entered pipeline and starts timing each stage.
Opportunity Contact Role Sync
Auto-creates contact roles whenever you name a Champion, Decision-Maker or Economic Buyer.

4
Acquisition · the pivot

Commit

The decisive stage of the bowtie. You drive the deal to close — the system handles the commercial maths, governance, forecasting and commission so the win is clean and auditable.

👤What you do
  • Advance through Negotiation → Commit, keeping Next Steps current.
  • Confirm the commercials (Recurring Revenue, term).
  • Close the deal Won — and pick a Closed Reason.
⚙️What the system does for you
Opportunity — Stage Change Governance
Blocks stage advance until the required fields for that stage are complete.
Deal Amount Validation + Populate ARR
Derives MRR · ARR · ACV · TCV from recurring revenue and term — no manual maths.
Weighted Forecast + Forecast Submission nightly rollup
Calculates weighted value and rolls your deals into the period forecast automatically.
Close-Won Velocity Freeze
Stamps the true Actual Close Date the instant you win.
Sales Commission Calculator
On Closed Won, computes tiered commission (5% / 8% / 12%) and logs a commission record.
Deal Approval Apex
Routes discount / terms approvals where required, then rolls the win up to the Account.
Next-Step Pipeline Hygiene daily
Creates follow-up tasks for overdue or upcoming next steps so deals never stall silently.

5
Retention & Growth

Onboard

The prospect is now a customer. The system rolls the win up to the account and seeds the renewal, so the relationship starts on a clean, measurable footing.

👤What you do
  • Hand off to delivery / Customer Success.
  • Run the onboarding kickoff (your Next Step is already set from the win).
  • Confirm the account record reads cleanly for the CS team.
⚙️What the system does for you
Opportunity — Create Renewal on Close Won
Generates the next-term Renewal opportunity automatically so it’s never forgotten.
Account Deal Summary Roll-up Apex
Aggregates won deals, amounts, first/last opportunity dates onto the Account in one bulk-safe pass.
Target Account Record Management & Summary Sync
If this is a strategic target account, stamps start dates and syncs the account summary.
💡 Tip: The roll-up fields on the account are your at-a-glance customer snapshot — they update every time a deal changes, with zero manual maintenance.
6
Retention & Growth

Impact

The customer realises value. The system measures engagement and attainment against targets, turning every meeting into a tracked, attributed signal of health.

👤What you do
  • Run QBRs and value reviews; log meetings & outcomes.
  • Watch account health and attainment dashboards.
  • Flag risks early; capture expansion signals.
⚙️What the system does for you
Activity Attribution Engine
Matches meetings to active Targets and credits “Meetings Booked / Held” KPIs.
Target Scorecard Status Engine Apex roll-up
Recalculates attainment vs target and sets a RAG performance status per target.
Scorecard Quota Sync Engine
Keeps quota figures aligned across targets and scorecards.
Recalculate Sales Velocity Benchmarks nightly
Refreshes win-rate & cycle-time benchmarks so “healthy” is always measured against current reality.
Event — Meeting Enrichment & Data Quality
Defaults and validates meeting fields (type, sentiment, outcome) so engagement data stays clean.

7
Retention & Growth

Grow

Expand and renew. The renewal the system seeded at the win is waiting for you, and expansion deals link back so revenue is never double-counted.

👤What you do
  • Work the auto-created Renewal opportunity ahead of term.
  • Raise expansion deals via the same New Opportunity button.
  • Run the renewal QBR; present expansion modules.
⚙️What the system does for you
Create Renewal on Close Won
The renewal opp already exists — pre-dated to next term, ready to forecast.
Link Expansion to Renewal
Ties expansion deals to the renewal so the account’s forward revenue stays coherent.
Update Renewal ARR on Expansion Close
When an expansion closes, the renewal’s ARR updates so the renewal reflects the larger footprint.
Renewal Duplicate Guard
Prevents two renewals being created for the same term.
🔎 Look for: the Renewal opp’s Deal Type = Renewal and a close date a full term out — your future pipeline, generated the day you won.
